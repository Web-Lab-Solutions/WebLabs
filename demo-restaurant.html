Our Story

A Passion for
Open-Fire Cooking

Founded in 2018, Ember & Oak brings modern British cooking with a focus on open-fire cooking and seasonal, locally sourced ingredients. Every dish tells a story of the land and the seasons.

Our chef works closely with farmers, foragers, and fishermen across the British Isles to bring you a menu that evolves with the calendar. From the crackle of the hearth to the final flourish on the plate, every detail matters.
