Our Story

Baking with heart
since the very beginning

We're a small, family-run bakery passionate about making real bread the way it should be. Every loaf is hand-shaped, every pastry rolled with care, and every cake decorated with love.

We source our flour from local mills, use real butter (never margarine), and add zero preservatives — just honest, artisan baking that you can taste.
