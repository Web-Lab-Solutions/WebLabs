About Me

Hello, I'm
Eleanor James

Photographer, natural light enthusiast, and eternal observer of beautiful moments. Based in London, working worldwide.

My Story

The journey so far

My love for photography began on a rain-soaked afternoon in 2016, armed with nothing but a secondhand camera and an overwhelming curiosity. What started as a quiet hobby — photographing friends, street scenes, the way light fell through my kitchen window — quickly became an all-consuming passion.

After studying fine art at university and spending two years assisting established photographers, I launched Lens & Light Studio in 2018. I wanted to create a space where photography felt less like a transaction and more like a collaboration — where clients felt seen, heard, and genuinely relaxed.

Eight years later, I've had the privilege of photographing over 500 sessions — from intimate elopements in the Scottish Highlands to editorial spreads for independent magazines. Every single shoot teaches me something new, and I wouldn't have it any other way.

Philosophy

My Approach

I believe the best photographs happen when people forget the camera is there.

Natural Light

I work almost exclusively with natural and ambient light. It's softer, more honest, and creates a mood that artificial lighting simply can't replicate.

Relaxed Sessions

No stiff poses or forced smiles. I guide you gently and let genuine moments unfold naturally. The result is photography that feels authentically you.

Storytelling

Every image should tell a story. I look for the in-between moments, the quiet glances, and the genuine emotions that make your story unique.

Gear

My Equipment

The tools of the trade. While gear doesn't make the photographer, using reliable, professional equipment ensures the highest quality results for every client.

Camera Body

Sony A7R V — 61 megapixels of detail, incredible dynamic range, and silent shooting for those quiet moments.

Prime Lenses

Sony 35mm f/1.4 GM — the storytelling lens. Sony 85mm f/1.4 GM — for portraits with beautiful compression. Sony 50mm f/1.2 GM — versatility and stunning bokeh.

Zoom Lenses

Sony 24-70mm f/2.8 GM II — the workhorse for weddings and events. Sony 70-200mm f/2.8 GM II — for capturing candid moments from a distance.

Lighting & Accessories

Godox AD200 Pro portable flash, MagMod modifier system, Gitzo carbon fibre tripod, and a carefully curated bag of reflectors and diffusers.

Space

The Studio

Based in a converted warehouse in East London, my studio is a creative space designed for comfort and inspiration. Large north-facing windows flood the space with beautiful natural light throughout the day.

It's equipped for portrait sessions, product photography, and small editorial shoots — with a selection of backdrops, props, and a comfortable client area with tea, coffee, and good music.
