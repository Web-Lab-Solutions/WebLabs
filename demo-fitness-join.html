Got Questions?

Frequently Asked

Can I cancel my membership at any time?
Absolutely. All our memberships are month-to-month with no long-term contracts. You can cancel at any time — just give us 30 days' notice and you're good to go. No cancellation fees, no hassle.
Do I need to be fit to join?
Not at all. We welcome all fitness levels, from complete beginners to seasoned athletes. Our coaches provide scaled options in every class, and our onboarding session will help you find the right starting point for your goals.
Is there a joining fee?
No. There's no joining fee, no admin fee, and no setup charges. Your first week is completely free as a trial, and if you decide to sign up, you simply start paying your monthly rate.
Can I freeze my membership if I'm away?
Yes. Pro and Elite members can freeze their membership for up to 4 weeks per year at no extra charge. Basic members can freeze for up to 2 weeks. Just let us know at least 7 days in advance.
What should I bring to my first class?
Just yourself, comfortable workout clothes, and a water bottle. We provide towels, all equipment, and have full locker rooms with showers. Arrive 10 minutes early so your coach can show you around and get you set up.
