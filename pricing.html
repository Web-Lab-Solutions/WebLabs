FAQ

Common questions

How long does it take to build a website?
Timelines depend on the project scope. A Starter site typically takes 1–2 weeks, a Professional site 3–4 weeks, and Enterprise projects 6+ weeks. We'll give you a clear timeline during our initial consultation.
Do you offer ongoing maintenance?
Yes! We offer optional maintenance plans starting from £49/month that include security updates, content changes, performance monitoring, and priority support.
Will I be able to update the site myself?
Absolutely. For our Professional and Enterprise packages, we integrate a user-friendly CMS so you can update text, images, and blog posts without touching any code.
What do you need from me to get started?
We'll need your brand assets (logo, colours, fonts if available), content (text and images), and a rough idea of what you want. Don't worry if you don't have everything — we can help with content and sourcing images too.
Do you register domains and provide hosting?
We can handle domain registration and recommend reliable hosting providers tailored to your needs. We'll set everything up so your site is live and accessible.
What if I'm not happy with the design?
We work iteratively — you'll see designs at each stage and provide feedback before we move forward. Each package includes revision rounds, and our collaborative process means there are no surprises at launch.
